Solar-Powered Education Initiative

Project Aim

Our initiative aims to transform education in rural Kenya by providing sustainable solar power solutions to schools. This will enable reliable access to digital learning tools, extended study hours, and improved educational outcomes for thousands of students. The project includes installation of solar panels, battery storage systems, and maintenance training for local staff.

Current Funding: $65,000 Target: $100,000
